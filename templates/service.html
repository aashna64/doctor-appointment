Heart Disease Prediction
Heart disease, a leading cause of death globally, encompasses conditions like coronary artery disease and heart attacks, often driven by risk factors such as high blood pressure, high cholesterol levels, and smoking. Lifestyle modifications including regular exercise, healthy diet choices, and smoking cessation can significantly reduce the risk of developing heart disease. Additionally, early detection through regular check-ups and screenings plays a crucial role in managing heart health.

Diabetes Prediction
Diabetes, a metabolic disorder affecting blood sugar regulation, poses significant health risks including cardiovascular disease, nerve damage, and kidney failure if left untreated.
Effective management of diabetes involves maintaining blood sugar levels through medication, diet, exercise, and regular monitoring. Education and support for individuals with diabetes are essential in preventing complications and improving overall quality of life..

Know your Diabetes Status

Breast Cancer Prediction
Breast cancer is one of the most common cancers among women worldwide, is influenced by various factors including genetic predisposition, hormonal influences, obesity, and alcohol consumption. Early detection through regular mammograms and breast exams can significantly improve treatment outcomes and survival rates. Advances in treatment modalities, including surgery, chemotherapy and radiation therapy offer hope for individuals diagnosed with breast cancer.
